Business Acquisition Evaluation

In this project, we built an end-to-end M&A evaluation pipeline that (1) predicts the likelihood of a target’s acquisition using machine learning, (2) estimates its intrinsic value via a multi-stage DCF model, and (3) quantifies post-merger synergies to produce a “with-synergies” equity value.

Context & Motivation

Mergers and acquisitions are critical for corporate growth, but target identification and valuation remain time-consuming and error-prone when done manually. By automating deal screening with AI and rigorous financial modeling, we enable faster, more data-driven decisions and help acquirers prioritize high-potential targets.

Technical Skills & Methods

Data Engineering & Feature Engineering (Python, SQL)

  • Unified Crunchbase records into a SQL schema.
  • Engineered predictors such as total funding, number of funding rounds, and founders’ educational credentials.

Acquisition-Likelihood Modeling (scikit-learn)

  • Imputed missing values and one-hot encoded categorical fields with DataFrameMapper & custom imputers.
  • Trained a GradientBoostingClassifier to output a probability score of an M&A event.
  • Identified top drivers (e.g., company age, founder degrees, funding history) via feature‐importance analysis.

Valuation Modeling (Excel & Python)

  • Built a multi-phase Discounted Cash Flow model: revenue growth projection, operating-margin evolution, WACC calculation, and terminal value.
  • Produced a detailed cash-flow schedule, DCF value, and implied per-share equity valuation.

Synergy Valuation

  • Modeled incremental cash flows from merger synergies and added them to the baseline DCF to derive a “with-synergies” valuation.

Results & Impact

Achieved a robust acquisition-likelihood classifier with clear, interpretable feature rankings (age ≈ 0.34 importance; advanced degrees ≈ 0.20). Delivered a scalable preprocessing pipeline that handles unseen categories and missing data, ensuring consistent scoring across new company records. Produced both standalone and synergy-adjusted valuations to guide strategic deal pricing, reducing manual effort and improving decision confidence.
